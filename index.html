About Me

About Me

Im a UX/UI & Product Designer with a global design background, having worked on projects across Italy, Dubai, and India. My journey began in architecture, where I learned how to balance aesthetics, functionality, and user needs while collaborating with diverse stakeholders on large-scale projects.


I thrive in collaborative, fast-paced environments and enjoy transforming challenges into opportunities for meaningful design. Whether its shaping products or interfaces, my goal is always the same: to create human-centered experiences that are usable, accessible, and impactful.


In my free time, I enjoy traveling, hiking, kayaking, and indulging in the relaxing pastime of sketching and painting.
